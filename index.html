About me

I am a UX, UI designer that studied a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Communication at The University of Reading, achieving a 2:1. I have worked as a freelance digital designer for the last 3 years and worked in house as a UX/UI designer for a lead generation company for the past year.

Having had a keen interest in design from a young age, I have developed my design skillset over several years and enjoyed applying my natural creativity in this field in a variety of differing contexts. Employers and clients comment that I am a quick learner, enthusiastic, and always willing to go the extra mile to achieve the right result.

Though I thrive working amongst a team of like-minded people, I’m ambitious and motivated enough to work to a high standard individually, and welcome a challenge.

Outside of design, I'm avidly into fitness, competing at a semi-professional level in powerlifting before the initial lockdown, and have also ran numerous charity 5k, 10k, etc..
